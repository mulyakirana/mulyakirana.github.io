The Impact of Allowing Students To Listen The Music While Learning

Listen to the music is one of populer activities for students. In fact, almost all of the students love to hear the music, besause it can produce the spirit of the brighter day and guarantee brings the enjoyable into their life. But how if it is apply by students while they are learning. Besides, while learning, students need to be focus and consentrate to digest the subject given by teachers. So,in this case will aprears the good impact and also the bad impact that will be exist in a particular situation. To observe the good impact of allowing students to listen the music while learning, the thing has to do is look at the kind of music or genre that playing. Because there is suggestopedia method in teaching method that facilitated a relaxed and comfortable environment with playing soft music, for instance, jazz, clasiccal, or instrumental music, in order to make students easy to receive the subject without pressure.

In fact, when the mind is relaxed students can focus more and anything they do will easier. Moreover, classical music is a great tool for increasing consentration and productively. Another good impact is students feel no borring when sometimes the teachers are monotoniously or have no other ideas to develop the subject. Furthermore, their boredom and emotion will taper off by listening to the music. And the students will ignore the problem of the teachers and just enjoy the show. On the other hand, almost all of teachers do not allowed the students to listen the music while learning, because of some bad impacts of it. Firsly, the students will hard of hearing the point of subject and the consetrate will smashed by the music. In the same way, not all students can approve music as learning aid, consequently they could not catch the point if there is something disturb them, like noisy or shrill even soft voices. Therefore it would be an obstacle for teachers to deliver their thought about the target subject. And automatically the teaching and learning prosess could not work as a good as in proper order.
